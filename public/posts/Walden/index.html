Margarete Walden

PhD Student at University of Nevada, Reno

October 15, 2016 Margarete Walden

Margarete is a Ph.D. student who joined the APE lab and the EECB program in Fall 2016. Her research interests include population ecology, herpetology, conservation biology, terrestrial vertebrate physiology, and species’ responses to climate change. She is completing her M.S. with Dr. Nancy Karraker on the timing of hibernation in Eastern Box Turtles.
