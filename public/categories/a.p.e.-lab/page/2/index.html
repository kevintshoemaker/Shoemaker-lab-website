Danielle C Miles

Doctoral Candidate - Ecology, Evolution, and Conservation Biology

October 4, 2016 Danielle C Miles

Danielle C Miles I am a PhD student in my first year here at UNR in the APE lab. I will be working on measuring the broad impacts of pinyon and juniper tree removal in sagebrush ecosystems on bat, bird, reptile, small mammal and insect populations. Besides this ecological study, I am interested in exploring anthropogenic effects on the environment such as climate change and deforestation with specific emphasis on the implications for amphibians.
