Mia Goldman completed her MS in the A.P.E. lab in 2022, where she studied the factors driving occupancy and detection patterns of American pika (*Ochotona princeps*) in the northwestern USA. Mia is currently a PhD candidate at the University of St Andrews in Scotland where she works with the Centre for Research into Ecological and Environmental Modelling (CREEM).
